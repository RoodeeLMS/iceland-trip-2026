üöó Rental Car Strategy

Vehicle Requirements

Recommended: 4WD/AWD vehicle (especially for F-roads if exploring)

Minimum: 2WD adequate for Ring Road in summer

Size: Small SUV or larger for comfort on long drives

Why 4WD is Beneficial:

  • Better handling on gravel roads (many parking areas unpaved)
  • Safer in inclement weather (wind, rain common)
  • Access to highland F-roads if desired (summer only)
  • Peace of mind on remote East Fjords sections

‚ö†Ô∏è Driving Conditions & Safety

Iceland Driving Challenges

üå™Ô∏è Wind (Most Common Hazard)

Risk: Extremely strong gusts can blow car doors off or push vehicles off road

Solution:

  • Hold door firmly when opening in wind
  • Park facing away from wind when possible
  • Drive slower on exposed sections (South Coast, highlands)
  • Check wind forecasts daily (vedur.is)

üêë Sheep on Roads

Risk: Free-roaming sheep, especially in summer

Solution: Drive slower on rural roads, always be ready to brake

üåâ Single-Lane Bridges

Risk: Common throughout Iceland, yield rules apply

Solution: Slow down, first to bridge has right of way

üö´ Gravel Roads & F-Roads

Risk: Unpaved roads, flying gravel, river crossings

Solution:

  • Slow down to 60-70 km/h on gravel
  • F-roads require 4WD and are closed until mid-June
  • Never attempt river crossings without experience

‚òÄÔ∏è Midnight Sun (Summer)

Risk: 24-hour daylight can disrupt sleep and sense of time

Solution: Bring eye mask, set alarms, don't rely on darkness cues

Essential Resources

  • road.is: Real-time road conditions and closures

  • safetravel.is: Safety information and registration
