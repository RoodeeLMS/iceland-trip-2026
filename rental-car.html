📸 Car Inspection Checklist (CRITICAL)

Before Driving Off — Photo/Video Checklist

  • 📷 All 4 sides of the car — zoom into any scratches, dents, chips
  • 📷 All 4 wheels & tires — condition, tread depth, any curb damage
  • 📷 Windshield — any chips or cracks, photograph from inside and outside

Tip: Do the SAME process at drop-off (Apr 17, 05:30). It's early and dark — use your phone flashlight. Ask staff to confirm "no damage" in writing or on camera.

🛡️ Platinum Protection — What's Covered

✅ Covered (€0 excess)

  • Collision damage (body, bumpers, panels)
  • Gravel damage (windshield, headlights, front paint)
  • Sand & ash storm damage
  • Tire & wheel damage/replacement
  • Animal collision (sheep, horses)
  • Theft protection
  • No security deposit required

❌ NOT Covered (even with Platinum)

  • Undercarriage/chassis damage — transmission, driveshaft, suspension
  • Water damage — river crossings (can cost €10,000+!)
  • Wind damage to doors — hinges, frame from gusts
  • Towing/recovery costs — stuck in snow, sand, mud
  • Driving intoxicated — all insurance voided
  • F-road violations — fine 350,000 ISK
  • Closed road violations — fine 100,000 ISK
  • Wrong fuel — full repair cost
  • Lost keys/equipment — replacement cost
  • Personal belongings inside car

🚗 Iceland Driving Rules & Tips

📏 Basic Rules

  • Drive on the RIGHT side (same as Thailand)
  • Speed limits: 30-50 km/h (town), 80 km/h (gravel), 90 km/h (paved highway)
  • Headlights ON at all times — mandatory 24/7
  • Seatbelts mandatory for all passengers
  • Zero tolerance for phone use while driving
  • Speed cameras are common and fines are steep

💨 Wind Safety (YOUR #1 RISK)

  • Always hold car doors firmly when opening — wind can rip them off the hinges (NOT covered by insurance!)
  • Park nose into the wind when possible
  • Open doors on the sheltered side first
  • If wind exceeds 20 m/s (72 km/h) — consider staying put
  • Check road.is and vedur.is wind map every morning

🪨 Gravel Road Tips

  • Slow to 60-70 km/h on gravel (limit is 80 but slower is safer)
  • Reduce speed when meeting oncoming traffic — flying rocks!
  • Keep distance from car ahead (rock spray)
  • Don't brake suddenly on gravel — pump brakes gently
  • Gravel-to-pavement transitions can be sudden — slow down

🌊 Water & River Crossings

  • NEVER cross rivers — not covered by ANY insurance
  • Water damage can cost €10,000+
  • F-roads with river crossings are closed in April anyway
  • Even puddles on roads — drive slowly to avoid water splash into engine

🐑 Animals on Roads

  • Sheep are less common in April (released June-Sept)
  • Icelandic horses may be near roads — slow down
  • Animal collisions ARE covered by your Platinum Protection
  • If you hit an animal: report to police + nearby farm

🅿️ Parking & Fuel

  • Most attractions have free parking (some charge ~ISK 750)
  • Fuel: ~ISK 350/liter (~฿88/liter). Full tank ~ISK 21,000 (~฿5,250)
  • Self-service pumps require chip+PIN credit card
  • N1, Orkan, OB, Olis — major chains everywhere on Route 1
  • Fill up in towns — long stretches with no stations in remote areas

🚫 Absolutely Prohibited (Insurance Voided)

Any of these actions void ALL insurance — you pay full damage cost:
  • Driving on F-roads (all closed in April anyway)
  • Crossing any river or watercourse
  • Driving off-road (off marked roads/tracks)
  • Driving on beaches or tidal areas
  • Driving on closed roads (check road.is daily!)
  • Driving under the influence of alcohol/drugs
  • Allowing an unregistered driver to operate the car
  • Driving on snow banks or ice outside the road

🆘 If Something Goes Wrong

🚨 Accident/Damage Protocol

  • Call police immediately: 112
  • Do NOT leave the scene until police arrive
  • Take photos of everything — damage, scene, other vehicles
  • Report to Icerental within 12 hours — late = CDW voided!
  • Phone: +354 571-2266
  • Get a police report number

🛞 Flat Tire / Breakdown

  • Check if spare tire/repair kit is in the car at pickup
  • Tire damage IS covered by Platinum Protection
  • But towing/recovery is NOT covered
  • Roadside assistance: +354 571-2266 (Icerental)
  • General roadside: +354 511-1111 (FIB)

🌬️ Stuck in Snow/Sand

  • Towing costs: 25,000-40,000 ISK base + 480 ISK/km
  • NOT covered by any insurance
  • Prevention: Don't drive off marked roads, check conditions
  • If stuck: Call Icerental first, then FIB
