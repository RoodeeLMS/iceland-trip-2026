📅 Iceland Itinerary 2026

10 days in Iceland + Berlin layover

✈️ Bonus Stop: Berlin

Bonus April 8 - Berlin Layover
▼

Morning (08:30)

  • Arrive at Berlin Brandenburg Airport (BER) at 08:30 from Doha
  • 5h 35m layover (08:30 - 14:05) — enough for a quick city visit!

Sightseeing Sprint (09:15 - 12:00)

  • Take the Airport Express (FEX) to Berlin Hauptbahnhof — 30 min, €3.80
  • Store luggage at Berlin Hbf lockers (€4-6)
  • Walk to Brandenburg Gate — just 15 min from Hauptbahnhof
  • See the Reichstag building (exterior — no time for dome visit)
  • Visit the Berlin Wall Memorial at Bernauer Straße
  • Quick stop at Checkpoint Charlie

Return to Airport (12:00 - 13:00)

  • Grab a quick Currywurst or Döner Kebab near Hauptbahnhof
  • Collect luggage and take FEX back to BER — arrive by 13:00 for 14:05 flight

Afternoon — Onward to Iceland

  • Depart BER at 14:05 on Icelandair FI529 to Keflavík (KEF)
  • Arrive KEF at 15:50 (same day)

📝 Practical Notes

  • Currency: Euro (€). Cards accepted almost everywhere
  • Luggage: Store at Berlin Hbf lockers (€4-6) — conveniently located in the station
  • Weather (April): 7-14°C, mild. Light jacket is enough
  • Timing is tight: Back to BER by 13:00 for the 14:05 Icelandair flight — don't linger!

🍽️ Food Stops

  • 🥨 Quick stop: Any Currywurst stand near Brandenburg Gate — ~฿250. Absolute Berlin classic and fast.
  • 🥖 Bakery: Any Backwerk / Le Crobag chain in the S-Bahn station area — cheap pastries + coffee while in transit.

  • Browse shops, street art, and cozy cafés

  • Icelandic Lamb Soup: A warming classic after a long travel day
  • Bæjarins Beztu: Famous hot dog stand — a Reykjavík institution
  • Fish restaurant: Fresh catch of the day at one of downtown's many seafood spots

20:30 - 21:00 — Krónan Supermarket

  • Krónan at Fiskislóð 1 — big grocery shop for Days 1-3 (open until 21:00)
  • Stock up on breakfast, snacks, drinks, and dinner supplies
  • Tip: Shop after dinner when it's dark — use daylight for sightseeing!

🌋 OPTIONAL: Lava Show Reykjavík — 20:00 show

  • The world's only live lava experience — real 1100°C basaltic lava poured in front of you. Winner of Tripadvisor Best of the Best 2025, 4.8 stars (4,252 reviews).
  • Showtime: 20:00 (also 10:00, 12:00, 14:00, 16:00, 18:00 daily)
  • Duration: ~50-60 minutes
  • Price: 6,590 ISK/adult (~฿1,648 · ~฿6,590 for 4)
  • Location: Grandi district, Reykjavík · free shuttle from city centre runs 11:50-15:46 (not useful at 20:00 — walk or drive ~15 min from the apartment on Vatnsstígur)
  • Book ahead: sells out — 3-5 days lead time on lavashow.com/reykjavik
  • Timing trade-off: If booking this, skip or speed through the Laugavegur stroll; eat dinner earlier (18:30) so you can leave for Grandi by 19:30. Or shift Krónan grocery shop to before the show.
  • ⚠️ Don't book if also doing Lava Show Vík Day 3 — same content, same operator, same lava pour. Pick one. See activities page for full comparison.

🌋 Return West & Snæfellsnes Peninsula (Days 5-8)

Day 5 April 12 - Ice & The Drive West
▼

☀️ Sunrise: ~06:11 | 🌙 Sunset: ~20:32

🗺️ View Day 5 Route on Google Maps

☀️ PHOTO DAY STRATEGY: Weather forecast shows dry/calm conditions on Apr 12 — the BEST weather on the South Coast! Days 3-4 going east had rain/snow. Today is your chance to revisit missed spots and get the photos you couldn't on the way out. Don't rush — treat this as a dedicated photography day.

Early Morning (06:00 - 08:30) — Sunrise Photography

  • 🌅 Vestrahorn / Stokksnes — The most iconic sunrise shot in Iceland! Leave your accommodation ~05:30. Dramatic mountain reflections on Stokksnes beach (1,000 ISK parking at Viking Cafe)
  • Alternative: Sunrise at Jökulsárlón — icebergs glowing pink/orange (if you don't want to drive to Vestrahorn)
  • Diamond Beach — Ice chunks on black sand in golden light. Arrive ~08:00 for low-angle light

Morning (08:30 - 12:00) — Return Drive, Photo Stops

  • Leisurely drive west. Stop at everything you missed going east!
  • Hofskirkja — Historic turf church in Öræfi. Picturesque in any weather
  • Dverghamrar — Dwarf cliffs, basalt column formations
  • Fjaðrárgljúfur Canyon — Dramatic canyon near Kirkjubæjarklaustur (if not closed)
  • Eldhraun lava field — Moss-covered lava from 1783 eruption (don't walk on the moss!)

Afternoon (12:00 - 18:00) — Revisit the South Coast

  • Lunch in Vík: Suður-Vík, The Soup Company, or Kronan for picnic supplies
  • Reynisfjara revisit — Black beach in better light than Day 3. Long shadows on basalt columns
  • Gljúfrabúi — The hidden canyon waterfall you might have skipped on Day 3 (near Seljalandsfoss)
  • Kvernufoss — Behind Skogar Museum. Walk-behind experience with 5% of the crowds
  • Skógafoss — Revisit in afternoon light for rainbow shots
  • Seljalandsfoss — Walk behind it at golden hour (18:00+) if timing allows

Evening (18:00 - 21:00) — Golden Hour & Dinner

  • Hidden Gem: Gluggafoss (Merkjárfoss) near Hvolsvöllur (Route 261). A unique waterfall flowing through natural "windows" in the cliff
  • Stora Mork area — Check into your cottage by 19:00-20:00
  • Dinner: Cook at cottage or Midgard Base Camp in Hvolsvöllur
  • 🌌 Aurora watch tonight — check cloud cover, this area has dark skies

🍽️ Where to Eat

  • Pakkhus (Hofn, if passing through) — Famous langoustine soup, worth the splurge! ~ISK 3,500 (~฿875) | Open 12:00-21:00
  • N1 gas stations along Route 1 — Lamb soup, hot dogs, sandwiches ~ISK 500-2,000 (~฿140-490)
  • Budget: Packed lunch from Vik groceries — long drive day, eat on the road
  • Dinner: Cook at Stora Mork 3 (has kitchen), or The Old Cowhouse near Hvolsvollur ~ISK 3,000-4,000 (~฿735-980)

▼

☀️ Sunrise: ~06:07 | 🌙 Sunset: ~20:35

🗺️ View Day 6 Route on Google Maps

✂️ Reykjadalur Hot River — SKIPPED: The group is not into swimming/hot springs. We're trading the 3-4 hour hot river hike for more photography time and scenic drives. Focus: waterfalls, geothermal areas, and photo spots en route to Borgarnes.

Morning (08:30 - 12:00) — Scenic Drive North

  • Leisurely breakfast at Stora Mork, pack up
  • Drive Hvolsvöllur → Reykjavik area (~1.5 hours)
  • Quick stop: Seljalandsfoss or Gluggafoss if you want one more South Coast photo before heading inland
  • Continue north via Hvalfjörður tunnel toward Borgarnes area

Afternoon (12:00 - 17:00) — Waterfalls & Geothermal

  • Hraunfossar — "Lava Falls" — water trickling from a lava field over ~900 meters. Unique and beautiful
  • Barnafoss — "Children's Falls" — dramatic waterfall right next to Hraunfossar. Both share one parking lot (free)
  • Deildartunguhver — Europe's most powerful hot spring (180 L/sec of boiling water). Great photography, free to view
  • Lunch: Settlement Centre Café in Borgarnes or Ölverk Pizza in Hveragerði (detour)
  • Optional: Glymur waterfall (Iceland's 2nd tallest at 198m) — 3-4 hour hike, only if weather is perfect

Late Afternoon (17:00 - 19:00) — Borgarnes Arrival

  • Arrive in Borgarnes — gateway to Snæfellsnes Peninsula
  • Stop at Bonus supermarket for groceries for Snaefellsnes days
  • Borgarnes Settlement Centre — Viking history museum (optional, 2 hours)
  • Check into Fossatun Sunset Cottage by 19:00

🍽️ Where to Eat

  • Ölverk Pizza & Brewery (Hveragerði, detour option) — Local favorite pizza ~ISK 2,500-3,500 (~฿630-875)
  • Bara Borgarnes — Burgers, fish & chips, lamb chops. Generous portions! ~ISK 2,500-4,000 (~฿630-980) | Tue-Sun from 16:00-22:00
  • Settlement Centre Cafe (Borgarnes) — Soup, sandwiches, coffee ~ISK 1,500-2,500 (~฿385-630) | Open 10:00-18:00
  • Budget: Bonus supermarket in Borgarnes for supplies

Day 10 April 17 - Departure via Frankfurt
▼

Early Morning — Departure from Iceland

  • Drive from Garður to KEF airport (~12 km, 10 min drive)
  • Return rental car and check in
  • Depart KEF at 07:20 on Icelandair FI520 to Frankfurt

🍽️ Where to Eat

  • Breakfast: Eat at Gardur Apartments before the ~05:00 drive to KEF
  • Frankfurt Airport (Terminal 2): Grab a meal during 3h10m layover ~฿385-700 for fast food/café
  • Doha Hamad Airport: Excellent food court if hungry during 2h35m connection
⏰ Early Departure: For 07:20 AM flight, plan to arrive at Keflavík Airport by 05:00 AM. The short drive from Garður makes this manageable.

Afternoon — Frankfurt Connection (3h10m layover)

  • Arrive Frankfurt (FRA) at 13:00
  • 3h10m connection at Frankfurt Airport Terminal 2
  • Grab a meal at the terminal, browse duty-free
  • Schengen transit: No additional immigration needed (Iceland → Germany are both Schengen)

Evening — Homeward Bound

  • Depart FRA at 16:10 on Qatar Airways QR068 (A350-900) to Doha
  • Connect to QR828 (B777-300ER) Doha to Bangkok
  • Arrive Bangkok April 18

📝 Practical Notes

  • Currency: Euro (€). Cards widely accepted at airport shops and restaurants
  • Layover is airport-only: With only 3h10m, stay in the terminal — no time for a city visit
  • Frankfurt Terminal 2: Good selection of restaurants, cafes, and duty-free shopping

🍽️ Food Stops

  • 🛫 Early morning: FI520 departs KEF 07:20 — grab breakfast at the airport (Joe & the Juice, Mathús) before security.
  • 🍕 Frankfurt layover: ~3h in FRA — T2 food court has multiple sit-down options if you get hungry between flights.
