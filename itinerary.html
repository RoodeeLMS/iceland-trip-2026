10-Day Iceland Itinerary

Complete day-by-day breakdown of our Iceland adventure

🏙️ Reykjavík & Golden Circle

Day 1 Arrival & Reykjavík Exploration
▼

🗺️ View Day 1 Route on Google Maps

📍 Plan

  • Arrive at Keflavík International Airport
  • Pick up rental car
  • Drive to Reykjavík (45 minutes)
  • Check into hotel
  • Explore downtown: Hallgrímskirkja Church, Harpa Concert Hall
  • Walk along the waterfront
  • Dinner at local restaurant (try Icelandic lamb or seafood)

🎯 Focus

Settle in, shake off jet lag, get oriented in the capital

🚗 Transport

Pick up rental car at airport | Drive: 45km to Reykjavík

🏨 Hotel

Reykjavík accommodation (2 nights)

Day 2 Golden Circle Tour
▼

🗺️ View Day 2 Route on Google Maps

📍 Plan

  • Þingvellir National Park - UNESCO site, continental drift
  • Geysir Geothermal Area - Strokkur geyser eruption
  • Gullfoss Waterfall - "Golden Falls"
  • Optional: Kerið Crater or Secret Lagoon hot spring
  • Return to Reykjavík for evening

🎯 Focus

Iceland's most famous attractions in one circuit

🚗 Transport

Self-drive Golden Circle | Total: ~300km round trip

🏨 Hotel

Reykjavík accommodation (night 2)

🌊 South Coast Adventure

Day 3 Waterfalls & Black Sand Beach
▼

🗺️ View Day 3 Route on Google Maps

📍 Plan

  • Depart Reykjavík heading east
  • Seljalandsfoss Waterfall - walk behind it
  • Skógafoss Waterfall - climb stairs for view
  • Reynisfjara Black Sand Beach - basalt columns, sea stacks
  • Dyrhólaey Peninsula viewpoint
  • Arrive in Vík area for overnight

🎯 Focus

Iconic South Coast waterfalls and dramatic coastal scenery

🚗 Transport

Drive east on Ring Road | ~180km

🏨 Hotel

Vík or nearby accommodation (1 night)

Day 4 Glacier Lagoon & Ice Caves
▼

🗺️ View Day 4 Route on Google Maps

📍 Plan

  • Drive to Skaftafell National Park
  • Short hike to Svartifoss waterfall (basalt columns)
  • Continue to Jökulsárlón Glacier Lagoon
  • Watch icebergs floating in the lagoon
  • Diamond Beach - ice chunks on black sand
  • Optional: Glacier ice cave tour (book in advance)
  • Overnight near Höfn

🎯 Focus

Glaciers and Iceland's most photogenic location

🚗 Transport

Continue east on Ring Road | ~200km

🏨 Hotel

Höfn or Jökulsárlón area (1 night)

⛰️ East Fjords & Northern Iceland

Day 5 East Fjords Scenic Drive
▼

🗺️ View Day 5 Route on Google Maps

📍 Plan

  • Drive through winding East Fjords
  • Stop at scenic viewpoints along the way
  • Visit Djúpivogur or Stöðvarfjörður fishing villages
  • Continue to Egilsstaðir area
  • Relax and prepare for tomorrow's activities

🎯 Focus

Scenic driving day through remote fjord landscapes

🚗 Transport

Long scenic drive | ~250km

🏨 Hotel

Egilsstaðir area (1 night)

Day 6 Lake Mývatn & Geothermal Wonders
▼

🗺️ View Day 6 Route on Google Maps

📍 Plan

  • Drive to Lake Mývatn area
  • Dimmuborgir lava formations - "Dark Castles"
  • Hverir geothermal area - bubbling mud pots
  • Grjótagjá cave (Game of Thrones filming location)
  • Mývatn Nature Baths - geothermal spa (less touristy than Blue Lagoon)
  • Optional: Dettifoss waterfall (Europe's most powerful)

🎯 Focus

Volcanic landscapes and geothermal activity

🚗 Transport

Drive to Mývatn region | ~170km

🏨 Hotel

Mývatn area or Akureyri (1 night)

Day 7 Akureyri & Whale Watching
▼

🗺️ View Day 7 Route on Google Maps

📍 Plan

  • Morning in Akureyri - "Capital of the North"
  • Akureyrarkirkja Church viewpoint
  • Botanical Gardens
  • Drive to Húsavík for whale watching tour (book ahead)
  • High chance of humpback whales and dolphins
  • Explore Húsavík Whale Museum

🎯 Focus

Northern Iceland's capital and whale watching

🚗 Transport

Akureyri to Húsavík | ~90km

🏨 Hotel

Akureyri or Húsavík area (1 night)

🏔️ West Iceland & Snæfellsnes

Day 8 Journey West
▼

🗺️ View Day 8 Route on Google Maps

📍 Plan

  • Long drive west across northern Iceland
  • Stop at Glaumbær turf houses (historical museum)
  • Drive through Skagafjörður valley
  • Optional: Hvítserkur rock formation
  • Arrive at Snæfellsnes Peninsula

🎯 Focus

Transition day with cultural stops

🚗 Transport

Long drive west | ~300km

🏨 Hotel

Snæfellsnes Peninsula area (2 nights)

Day 9 Snæfellsnes Peninsula Circuit
▼

🗺️ View Day 9 Route on Google Maps

📍 Plan

  • Kirkjufell Mountain - most photographed mountain in Iceland
  • Kirkjufellsfoss waterfall in foreground
  • Djúpalónssandur black pebble beach
  • Snæfellsjökull Glacier viewpoints
  • Arnarstapi coastal cliffs and rock arch
  • Búðakirkja black church
  • Ytri-Tunga seal colony

🎯 Focus

"Iceland in Miniature" - diverse landscapes in compact area

🚗 Transport

Loop around peninsula | ~200km

🏨 Hotel

Snæfellsnes Peninsula (night 2)

🏁 Final Day

Day 10 Blue Lagoon & Departure
▼

🗺️ View Day 10 Route on Google Maps

📍 Plan

  • Drive back toward Reykjavík/Keflavík
  • Blue Lagoon geothermal spa (BOOK IN ADVANCE!)
  • Relax in milky blue waters
  • Silica mud mask included
  • Drive to Keflavík Airport
  • Return rental car
  • Depart Iceland

🎯 Focus

Relaxation and reflection before departure

🚗 Transport

Return to Keflavík Airport | ~150km

🏨 Hotel

N/A - Departure day
