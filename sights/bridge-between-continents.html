📖 Overview

The Bridge Between Continents (Brú milli heimsálfa, also called Leif the Lucky Bridge) is a small wooden footbridge spanning a narrow rift between the North American and Eurasian tectonic plates on Reykjanes. It is a 5-minute photo stop on Route 425, on the way between Gunnuhver / Valahnúkamöl and the Blue Lagoon. Mildly gimmicky — the rift here is just one of many on Reykjanes — but free, well-signed, and unavoidable since it is right on the route.

🚦 Current Status

🟠 Heads-up: Per safetravel.is reporting, areas around the bridge have seen subsidence and small sinkhole activity linked to ongoing seismic stress on the Reykjanes peninsula (2021-2024 eruption sequence). The bridge itself is reported safe but stay on marked paths and do not wander off-trail into the surrounding lava field.

April 2026: Open and free. Check safetravel.is the morning of for any updated alerts on the broader Reykjanes peninsula.

📷 Photography & Drone

Best angle: From the rift floor below the bridge, looking up — wide 16-24mm with the bridge spanning the frame and sky behind. The straight-on side view from the parking lot is the standard shot but less interesting.

Best light: Anytime — the rift runs roughly N-S so the bridge is well-lit most of the day.

🚫 Drone: Open Reykjanes terrain, no specific posted restrictions, but mind the seismic-zone advisory on safetravel.is. See Planning page.

🚗 Getting There

  • Paved Route 425. Any car.
  • Free gravel parking, ~20 spaces.
  • 50 m flat walk to the bridge.
  • Free, no fees.

🗺️ Itinerary & Timing

  • Time needed: 5-10 min.
  • Combine with: Gunnuhver (5 min S), Valahnúkamöl (10 min S), Brimketill (5 min S), Blue Lagoon (25 min E).
  • Honest assessment: Mildly gimmicky — geologically the rift is real but unimpressive compared to Þingvellir's Almannagjá. Worth the 5 minutes only because it is right on your route. Do not detour for it.

⭐ Reviews & Reputation

Google ~4.0 — notably lower than other Reykjanes stops. Reviewers consistently call it "tourist gimmick" and "5 min stop max" but also "free and on the way so why not." The lower rating is honest: expectations should be calibrated.

🎟️ Activities & Providers

Self-guided — no booked activities needed.

⚠️ Safety Notes

  • Per safetravel.is: subsidence / small sinkhole activity has been reported in the surrounding lava field. Stay on marked paths only.
  • Reykjanes seismic zone — check safetravel.is the morning of your visit.
  • The bridge railings are low; supervise small children.
